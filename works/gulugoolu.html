Gulu Goolu

Guloo goolu's name is inconsistent and unattributed to a single spelling. The city tells a story, and with the lack of land, nothing can be set in stone. All that extends are the oral traditions of what has happened and where we are now. Gulu Goolui, air through water, water through air, never in equilibrium.
