Rehabilitative Machine

2070, digitalisation, a conformist society alongside societal pressure are breeding ground for a reclusive society. Despite the change in societal behaviour, there is still an ingerent desire to return to society. The architecture attempts to be a passive rehabilitatibe housing model to encourage the reintegration back into society through the activity of urban farming, thereby encouraging social interactions.
