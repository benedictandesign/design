Neural Artefact Black

Neural Artefact Black marks itself as an act of reimagning the idea of restoration. By training a 3D generative model from the Singapore's National Collection of furnitures, the resolved form represents the generative archival. Past is restored to be made as the present artefact, formed from the historical context of its locality.

Commissioned by the Artshouse, it situates itself between the Asian Civilisation Museum and the Singapore River.
