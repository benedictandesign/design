BT/JK Crescendo

2070, digitalisation, a conformist society and social pressure are breeding ground for a reclusive society. Despite the change in societal behaviour, there is still an inherent desire to return to society.
The architecture tries to be a passive rehabilitative housing model to gradually reintegrate the reclusive individual back into society through the activity of urban farming, enabling social interactions to be developed.
