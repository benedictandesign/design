Before, During, After

Three connected stages of meditation, each setting the stage for the next to unfold smoothly.

Before. We pause and ease our thoughts, settling into a calm space and gently preparing the mind for stillness.

During. We turn inward, observing our reflections and letting each breath guide us through quiet contemplation.

After. We close the session with gentle care, preserving the sense of calm so the world beyond doesn’t intrude too quickly.

A distraction-free setting and a seamless shift from one stage to the next help the whole process flow naturally.
