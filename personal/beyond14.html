Beyond 14!

Spaces as composition of a scene in a photograph; some spaces become the subject, while others are the backdrop. Where photos capture a moment in time, the experience of architecture is a composition of moments in volumetric space.

Given 14 spaces that are related to each other in a visual network system, there are numerous ways to compose the spaces together.

By defining the location and rotation of these sapces, there are countless ways to create the desired netork of spaces.

Machine learning was used to explore the solution space of possible arrangements that offers visual optimisation of the network.
