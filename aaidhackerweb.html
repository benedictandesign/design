Pre-Automatism: History Untold

        
2030: As the state of the climate is accelerating to its doom, activist and corporation alike underwent a peaceful takeover of the then current government seeing that partisanship created soft policies. 

        
2035: Without governmental regulations, capitalism in its purest form maniffests. Companies are able to accelerate their technological advancements to the point that global environmental issues can be tackled head on. 

        
2040: Environmental conditions improves. A post work culture arises as automation becomes more pervasive and replaces almost every job.

        
2050: Humans are no longer part of the production/generative process. We were no longer part of the capitalistic process. Machines took over our land. Buildings were made for them instead. We exist just to consume.

        
2055: We are not needed by the corporations. Machines provide all that they want. Living standards drop to minimize resources comsumed by us. To quell our dissatisfaction and prevent a rebellion, machine intelligence was used to observe us, predict our movements and thereby influence our thoughts.

        
2060: No one remembers the life before. Our thoughts are no longer ours. We thought. therefore we were.
